Click Foundry release notes

r1.0.1 Factory Flow

Gameplay-facing changes for the public build. Share the PDF version as click-foundry-r1.0.1-patch-notes.pdf.

Patch Notes

  • LV chemistry now begins with the Air Collector and Centrifuge, producing Oxygen and Nitrogen for advanced processing and improved steel and aluminium programs.
  • Sulfur chemistry now progresses through Sulfuric Acid and Diluted Sulfuric Acid, supporting circuit-board production and future chemical processing.
  • Sticky Resin now separates into Rubber Pulp and Glue. Rubber Pulp supports Liquid Rubber and direct Rubber Bar production, while Glue improves resistor and multiblock-component assembly.
  • The recipe browser now lists autonomous fluids, gases, and every byproduct as obtainable outputs, including Air and Nitrogen, with clearer machine and ingredient navigation.
  • The LV quest book now branches through separation, gases, sulfur chemistry, and useful optional guidance instead of presenting one narrow route.
  • Hoppers now reliably pull from chests, furnaces, Auto Miners, and machine outputs, preserve transfer progress, and clearly distinguish input and output faces.
  • Factory maintenance now shows configured machine-automation directions, the LV Air Collector appears in placement and machine views, and startup artwork is preloaded again.
  • Formed multiblocks can be disassembled from their controllers, and removing an LV Auto Miner safely returns its installed Survey Card.
