r1.1.2 Factory Routing Fixes

Gameplay-facing changes for the public build. Share the PDF version as click-foundry-r1.1.2-patch-notes.pdf.

Patch Notes

  • Iron and Steel Tanks now supply water through configured pipe networks so Solid Fuel Boilers can run reliably from stored water.
  • LV Battery Buffers now accept EU on three faces and export through one selected face; existing saves migrate their routing safely.
  • Permanent imprint dies are now cut in the Circuit Imprinter, while the LV Compressor remains responsible for Hardened Die Blanks.
  • Machine fluid slots now support withdrawing from inputs, returning fluids to inputs, and topping partially filled Steel Cells up to 8L; output slots remain withdrawal-only.
  • Gold and Resonant Quartz veins now remain hidden until the Diamond Pickaxe tier, and the Home screen includes a Report Bug link with build details.
